Welcome the to the GPA and Grade Calculator!

This page was created to help college students to be able to calculate their GPA and grades. I noticed that non-traditional students as well as students who struggled with math had a hard time figuring out what their grades were for individual classes. Most of them also did not know or understand how to calculate a GPA. So as I went from one programming class to another I decided to create something that would help the other students calculate their grades and a GPA.

To be able to use the site you must register. Once you have registered can use all of the features of the site. If you create a profile it will help me in the research portion of the site.
